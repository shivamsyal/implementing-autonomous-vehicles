Revolutionizing the Road

Our road has remained relatively unchanged for the past decades, and innovation is due. Our transportation is growing, and is becoming a larger part of our world with each year that passes. Implementing autonomous cars is a step into a brighter future. These revolutions in technology and in our society are pushing the limits of human creativity and engineering. Today, scientists, engineers, and people around the world are working to bring the future to us. Introducing, autonomous vehicles.

How does this technology work?

Self driving vehicles are created primarily using software in association with LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging). LiDAR is a technology that allows autonomous vehicles to "see" what is happening around them, and paired with software, allows for the vehicle to recognize different objects, including traffic signs, traffic lights, other cars, speed limits and bumps, people, and cyclists. After processing this data, the software in the car decides to brake, accelerate, turn in relation to the final destination, or deploy airbags.
